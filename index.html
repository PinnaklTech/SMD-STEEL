About Us

SMD STEEL

Rarely do you come across individuals who share the same level of passion, commitment, and drive to take care of customers, while forming a relationship that goes beyond a simple business transaction. This shared vision among professionals from diverse backgrounds led to the formation of SMD Steel, LLC.

With over 100 years of combined experience in the metal and transportation industries, our team utilizes state-of-the-art steel processing equipment and an expansive metal storage facility. Combined with deep knowledge in fabrication and metallurgy, we offer a complete supply chain solution tailored for every customer. Visit us at our Mississippi location or follow us on LinkedIn for more.

Sustainability

At SMD Steel, we are committed to a sustainable future. Our energy-efficient plate processing equipment includes an automated fume extraction system to safely capture smoke generated during plasma cutting.

Our 35-acre facility retains over half its site with natural trees to combat greenhouse gases. Strategically placed recycling bins encourage team members to recycle, reducing landfill use. Every small step contributes meaningfully to carbon neutrality, leaving behind a healthier planet for future generations.
