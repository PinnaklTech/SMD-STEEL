Processing

Our top-of-the-line K5000xmc is a heavy-duty combination plasma cutting, oxy-fuel cutting, and milling machine that will give our customers a competitive edge over other plate burning machines. With multiple functions (cutting, milling, drilling) in a single machine, there’s no need for complex fixtures, multiple setups, or moving work pieces from station to station, maximizing productivity.

  • Plasma cutting up to 3” thick / Oxy Fuel cutting up to 8” thick
  • Beveling – Plasma & Oxy Fuel
  • Pipe cutting
  • Machining Capabilities
  • Drilling up to 4” hole
  • Tapping up to 2″ hole
  • Milling, Counter Boring, Chamfering, Hole Interpolation, Thread Milling
  • Laser plate alignment pointer for quick setting up the parts for nesting & cutting
  • Table Size 128″ x 500″
  • Automated tool changes in under 3 seconds
  • Percussion Marking System
